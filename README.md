 # TASK_01: Business Sales Performance Analytics

## 🏢 Company
**Future Interns** - Data Science & Analytics Internship Program

## 📌  TASK Overview
This Task analyzes a retail superstore's sales data to uncover revenue trends, identify top-performing products, and evaluate regional profitability.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Python** (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- **Google Colab** (Jupyter Notebook)
- **Dataset**: Superstore Sales Dataset

## 📊 Analysis Performed
1. Sales by Category
2. Monthly Sales Trend
3. Profit by Region
4. Top 10 Products by Profit

## 💡 Key Business Insights
- Different product categories show varying sales performance
- Sales fluctuate throughout the year with seasonal patterns
- The West region contributes the most to overall profit
- A small number of products drive the majority of profits
